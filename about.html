About Joechris Care Ltd

Premier Health & Social Care Staffing Agency in the UK

Joechris Care Ltd operates as a trusted staffing partner within the UK Health & Social Care sector. Trading as Lastminute Care & Nursing (Glasgow Branch), we provide highly qualified care professionals to care homes, nursing homes, and community support services nationwide.

We focus on quality, dignity, and safety — delivering flexible, dependable, and fully compliant staffing solutions tailored to each organisation’s needs.

Our Mission

At Joechris Care Ltd, our mission is simple: to elevate staffing standards in Health & Social Care through professionalism, integrity, and a genuine commitment to excellence.

Every placement we make is driven by our dedication to improving care outcomes and supporting organisations in delivering exceptional services.

We understand the importance of reliable staffing in delivering exceptional care. Our services are designed to support your organisation, your team, and those in your care—ensuring every individual receives the attention and professionalism they deserve.
