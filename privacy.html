Privacy Policy

How We Protect and Use Your Information

Quick Summary: We use your information to operate our website and mobile app, manage workforce scheduling, communicate with you, and keep our systems secure. We do not sell personal data. You can read our full Privacy Notice to understand how we use your information and how to exercise your rights.

Introduction

Last Minute Nursing (Glasgow Branch) ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your privacy and handling your personal information in a transparent, secure, and lawful manner.

This Privacy Notice explains how we collect, use, share, store, and protect personal information when you:

  • Visit or use our website
  • Register for or use our mobile application
  • Communicate with us in connection with our services

This notice applies to all users, including workers, staff, applicants, and visitors to our website and app.

We comply with the UK General Data Protection Regulation (UK GDPR) and the Data Protection Act 2018.

Who We Are

  • Data Controller: Last Minute Nursing (Glasgow Branch) ("Controller" for the purposes of UK GDPR)
  • Contact for privacy matters and rights requests: info@joechriscare.com
  • Data Protection / Information Governance Contact: manager@joechriscare.com
  • Postal Address: 100 Spaces West George Street, Glasgow, G2 1PB

Scope of This Privacy Notice

This Privacy Notice covers:

  • Our public website
  • Our mobile application
  • Related communications (email, SMS, in-app notifications)
  • System and security logs associated with the website and app

It does not cover third-party websites or services linked from our site or app. If you follow a link to another website, that provider's privacy notice will apply.

What Our Website and App Do

Our website and mobile application are designed to:

  • Enable workers to register and manage their profile
  • Allow users to submit availability and receive shift notifications
  • Support workforce scheduling and allocation
  • Communicate operational updates and confirmations
  • Maintain audit trails to ensure service integrity and security

The app and website are administrative and operational tools. They do not store clinical records or client health notes.

Personal Information We Collect

We collect only the information necessary to operate our services effectively and lawfully.

5.1 Information You Provide

Account and Contact Information:

  • Full name
  • Worker or employee ID
  • Email address
  • Phone number(s)

Scheduling and Workforce Information:

  • Role, competencies, and relevant clearances
  • Availability windows
  • Preferred locations or travel zones (not precise home address)
  • Shift allocation history
  • In-app messages and notifications

5.2 Information We Generate Automatically

Technical and Security Information:

  • App and website usage logs
  • Device and access logs
  • Audit trails showing actions taken within the system (who did what and when)

5.3 Information From Internal Systems

  • HR, compliance, or training systems used to confirm eligibility, competencies, or clearances

Special Category Data

We do not require special category data to use the website or app.

Where equal opportunities information (such as ethnicity) is collected:

  • It is optional
  • It is not used for shift allocation
  • It is stored separately with additional safeguards
  • It is processed only where a valid legal condition applies (for example, consent or employment law obligations)

Information We Do Not Collect: We do not record clinical notes or client medical information. We do not store free-text health data. Client requirements are handled using coded operational indicators only (for example, "female carer required").

Where We Get Your Information From

We collect personal information from:

  • You, when you register, update your profile, or use the website or app
  • Our internal HR or compliance systems
  • System-generated logs created through website and app activity

Why We Use Your Information and Our Legal Bases

We process personal information only where we have a lawful basis under UK GDPR.

Purpose Examples Legal Basis
Providing and managing our services Account creation, shift visibility, notifications, access logging Contract (Article 6(1)(b))
Workforce planning Matching availability and competencies, fair rotation, and allocation resolving queries Contract (Article 6(1)(b)) and/or Legitimate Interests (Article 6(1)(f))
Communications Emails, SMS, and in-app notifications about shifts or changes Contract (Article 6(1)(b))
Security and compliance Audit logs, system monitoring, fraud and misuse prevention Legitimate Interests (Article 6(1)(f)) and legal obligations

Note: Where special category data is processed, this is done only under an appropriate additional legal condition.

Automated Decision-Making

We use rules-based matching to help propose suitable shift assignments.

  • Decisions are not solely automated
  • Human review is applied in tie-breaks and edge cases
  • Users may request a human review, contest an outcome, or ask for an explanation

We provide a clear in-app explanation showing the main factors considered when proposing a shift.

Who We Share Information With

We share personal information only where necessary and under appropriate contractual safeguards.

11.1 Service Providers

  • Cloud hosting and databases (Microsoft Azure, UK region where available)
  • Messaging providers for email, SMS, and push notifications
  • IT support and system maintainers, with restricted and monitored access

11.2 Legal and Regulatory Sharing

We may disclose information where required by law, regulation, or lawful request from public authorities.

We do not sell personal data.

International Transfers

Our primary hosting is within the UK.

Where limited access from outside the UK is required:

  • UK-approved safeguards (such as the International Data Transfer Agreement or Addendum) are used
  • A documented transfer risk assessment is completed

You may contact us for details of current safeguards.

How Long We Keep Information

We retain personal information only for as long as necessary.

Data Type Retention Period
Profile and HR-relevant records Duration of engagement plus up to 6 years
Availability and allocation history Up to 24 months, then anonymised or deleted
Security and audit logs 24–36 months
Backups Short rolling restore window (typically 35–90 days)

Retention may be extended where required for complaints, legal claims, or regulatory obligations.

How We Keep Information Secure

We use layered technical and organisational security measures, including:

  • Role-based access controls
  • Multi-factor authentication
  • Encryption in transit and at rest
  • Secure key management
  • Environment segregation (no production data in test environments)
  • Continuous monitoring and logging
  • Regular vulnerability management and penetration testing
  • Strict supplier security requirements

All website and app activity is logged for security and audit purposes.

Your Data Protection Rights

You have rights under UK GDPR, including the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Request erasure
  • Restrict processing
  • Object to processing based on legitimate interests
  • Receive your data in a portable format

You also have rights related to automated decision-making, including requesting human intervention.

How to Exercise Your Rights

You can exercise your rights by emailing info@joechriscare.com

We aim to respond within one month. We may request identity verification where necessary.

Complaints

If you are concerned about how we handle your personal information, please contact us first so we can address the issue.

You also have the right to complain to the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO):

  • Website: ico.org.uk
  • Telephone: 0303 123 1113 (UK)

Changes to This Privacy Notice

We may update this Privacy Notice to reflect changes in our processing activities or legal requirements.

Material changes will be communicated via our website and, where appropriate, in-app notifications.
